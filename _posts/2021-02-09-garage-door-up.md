One of my favorite ways that creative people communicate is by "working with the garage door up," to riff on a passage from Robin Sloan. This is the opposite of the Twitter account which mostly posts announcements of finished work: it's giving a lecture about the problems you're pondering in the shower; it's thinking out loud about the ways in which your project doesn't work at all.

I love this kind of communication personally, but I suspect it also creates more invested, interesting followings over the long term.
